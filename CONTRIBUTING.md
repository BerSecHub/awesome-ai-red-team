# Contributing to Awesome AI Red Team

Thanks for helping keep this the sharpest offensive-AI resource list on the internet. 🔴

This is an **offense-first** list for **authorized** red teaming, security research, and CTFs. Keep that framing.

## What belongs here

- **Tools you actually run** — scanners, jailbreak frameworks, payload crafters, agentic/MCP exploit kits, offensive LLMs, MitM/recon tooling.
- **Labs, CTFs, and vulnerable apps** for hands-on practice.
- **Techniques & foundational papers** — only if they introduce a method people use or are canonical reading.
- **Datasets, benchmarks, payload collections.**
- **People, blogs, newsletters, communities** worth following.
- **Defenses** — only as **_bypass targets_**, tagged as such.

## What does NOT belong

- SEO blogspam, "top 10 AI tools" listicles, content-marketing posts.
- Dead, archived, or unmaintained repos (unless historically seminal).
- Duplicate entries — each resource lives in exactly one section (its best home).
- Vendor pages with no substance (the **Vendor & Market Landscape** section is the only home for pure company links).

## Format

Add your entry as a single bullet, in the right category, matching the house style:

```
- **[Name](https://canonical-url)** — one line, operator voice, on *why it matters* (not a marketing blurb).
```

Rules:

- **Canonical URL** — official repo/site over mirrors or news write-ups.
- **One line.** If you can't explain the value in a sentence, it's probably not sharp enough.
- Keep the **operator voice**: direct, technical, no fluff.
- Tag defenses with `*Bypass target.*` at the start of the description.
- Rough "most useful first" ordering is fine — strict alphabetical is not required.

## How to submit

1. Fork the repo.
2. Add your line(s) to the correct section.
3. Open a PR with a one-sentence rationale. If it's a tool, link a repo with real activity; if a paper, link the abstract.

Maintainers may reword descriptions for consistency. By contributing you agree your addition is released under the repo [LICENSE](LICENSE).

## Code of Conduct

Be direct, be technical, stay respectful and on-topic. Harassment or off-topic noise gets closed.
